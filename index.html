What is SBTCVM?

SBTCVM is a project to develop a usable balanced ternary virtual machine,
as well as develop the related tools, apis, programming languages, libraries and so on.

SBTCVM Mark 2 - The latest and greatest VM developed by the SBTCVM project.
It includes a variety of programs, and several utilities, such as a command shell, and a custom assembler.
